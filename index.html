Ke Gu

Professor | Beijing University of Technology

ABOUT

Professor Ke Gu, currently serving at the Beijing University of Technology, is an esteemed researcher and educator in Information Technology. With a rich background from Shanghai Jiao Tong University and a stint as a Research Fellow at Nanyang Technological University, Singapore, his work is well recognized globally. Ke Gu holds notable editorial roles in prestigious journals like IEEE and Entropy, contributing significantly to fields like image processing and multimedia. His research excellence is further highlighted by his accolades such as being a Highly Cited Researcher by Clarivate Analytics and receiving multiple best paper awards. His scholarly impact is profound, with an impressive h-index and numerous citations, underlining his profound influence in the scientific community.
